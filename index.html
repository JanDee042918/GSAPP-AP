GSAPP-AP

Based on a previous survey of the 2012 Masters of Architecture class, the GSAPP - ALUMNI PROJECT attempts to capture the state and evolution of GSAPP Alumni five years out. By comparing and contrasting the different disciplines and classes, it provides insight into GSAPP classes and helps record the paths GSAPP graduates have taken.
